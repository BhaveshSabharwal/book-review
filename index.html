Today's Pick

The Last Lecture

Randy Pausch
4.2 Stars




Kinjal Parekh March 27 2023
"The Last Lecture" by Randy Pausch is a heartfelt reflection on life and resilience in the face of terminal illness. Pausch's wisdom and optimism shine through his stories of pursuing dreams and cherishing relationships. This book is a poignant reminder to live with gratitude and purpose.
